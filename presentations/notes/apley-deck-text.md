Data standardization
If large scale data aggregation is to be conducted, the responsibility for data standardization must be moved to the data submitters.
Continuous auditing of a random, representative sample would be necessary.
We may be best served by working on data standardization in anticipation of reporting requirements from markets or legislation.
Private industry (both electronic data services and the food animal industries) will find the most efficient methods to provide any required data.
