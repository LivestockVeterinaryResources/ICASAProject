# Papers pulled for "An Overview of Outcomes" — index
Source: Nora Schrag Google Scholar (user=IpWT34sAAAAJ). Extractions saved here for pulling concrete examples.

## Extracted in full (open access) — the goldmine set
- **P13** benchmarks-disease-outcome-2022.md — Front Vet Sci 2022. CENTERPIECE. Regimens + disease pressure + 30-day outcome (Success/Relapse/Sold/Died). Contains the death-vs-retreatment decomposition (Fig 5) and within-farm trend examples. **THE paper for this talk.**
- **P15** multiple-metrics-feedyards-2023.md — Front Vet Sci 2023. Proof a single metric is uninterpretable: 4/14 feedyards ranked both top-4 and bottom-4 depending on metric.
- **P12** metaphylaxis-health-amr-2024.md — J Anim Sci 2024. Bridge to Bruce/AMR: use↑, health↑, resistance not selected; resistance readouts are method/study-dependent.
