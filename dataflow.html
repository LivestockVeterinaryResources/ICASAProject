Data flow map

How the numbers reach the site. Data moves in the direction of the copper arrows: raw feedyard files → the ICASA processing pipeline → the anonymized ICASA-anonymous engine → three Shiny apps → three published apps on shinyapps.io → the ICASAProject website. Boxes with no arrows are project folders that sit outside the live pipeline.
